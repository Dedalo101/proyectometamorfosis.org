## Welcome to Proyecto Metemorfosis

We create **one-of-a-kind silk garments** from consciously sourced Indian silk. Each piece is a wearable transformation — a bridge between ancient textile traditions and modern conscious living.

Our shirts, kurtas, and bespoke designs celebrate:
- **Ethical sourcing** — silk that honors the silkworm and the artisan
- **Timeless patterns** — hand-block printed paisleys, florals, and sacred geometry
- **Bespoke craftsmanship** — made-to-measure for your unique form and energy
- **Holistic values** — clothing that feels as good as it looks
